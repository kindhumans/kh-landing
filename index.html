Hello Kind Humans!

It is with mixed emotions that we announce that Kindhumans is closing its doors, after more than 7 years. It’s been a life-changing journey, and we couldn’t have done it without all of you amazing humans. Given unforeseen personal and professional challenges, it is time for us to spread kindness in other ways. We still believe in the mission, and are beyond grateful to all of our customers, employees, supporters, nonprofit partners, and everyone that has supported us along the way! KIND THANKS TO YOU ALL!

The impact that we have made with you, our kind community, fills our hearts with pride and joy.

Together we:

  • Provided 200,000 gallons of safe drinking water for families in Flint, Michigan
  • Shared 35,000 hours of mentorship for at-risk youth
  • Planted over 7,000 mangrove trees to combat climate change
  • Rallied behind meaningful events, like the Paddle for Unity, where we gained the in-person support of thousands during the height of the pandemic
  • Supported Mother Earth through work with Farmer’s Footprint, SeaTrees, Wildcoast, One Flower Project, and the National Forest Foundation, leaving a lasting environmental impact

For this, we extend our open arms, filled with gratitude to our kind community of over 50,000 kind humans! 

Though we are closing our doors we are keeping our hearts wide open for love, kindness and that positive part of human culture that really carries us all through life. We hope you do the same.

Be inspired and try your best to keep it kind!

- The Kindhumans Team

Please note, all existing orders will be fulfilled. Any questions or concerns can be addressed to info@kindhumans.com.
